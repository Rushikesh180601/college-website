Home

Saraswati College is affiliated with Sant Gadge Baba Amaravati University, Amaravati. The Institution pioneered leadership in quality education and research with a focus on technology. Over a period of three decades, about 10,000 students graduated from the Institution with Undergraduate and postgraduate Degrees in Computer Application and Management streams.

About Us

Vision

“To impart value-based quality education in technology and management domain in innovative way empowering students to serve the global society at large.”

Mission

  • To provide state-of-the-art infrastructure, amenities, dedicated staff, recreation, and sports, and an industry-based syllabus to nurture the academic needs.
  • To organize and conduct value-based technical, management, and spiritual workshops/programs to meet the upcoming challenges of the industry and society.
  • To arrange and conduct personality development programs for students to enhance their communication and presentation skills to develop their leadership qualities.
  • To develop our institute as a global IT hub where the world looks for solutions to their problems/needs through our research.
